# Arcane Brotherhood
Organisation dealing in the arcane that [[Rotom Moonbane|Rotom]] was part of. She didn't tell me a lot about this organisation yet. Stole some moonstone keys from [[Vellynne Harpell]].

We met Vellynne Harpell in [[Deep Delver's Enclave]], where she was investigating [[Shadowreach]]. Vellynne's a member of the Arcane Brotherhood, a collective of mages researching in all kinds of fields.
